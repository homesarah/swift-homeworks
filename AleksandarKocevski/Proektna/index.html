About


Born 1993 October in Skopje, Macedonia, currently studying and working in Sofia, Bulgaria, mostly focused on illustrating and creating various characters, 3D assets and designs for the game industry.

Holder of bachelors in "Poster and visual communication" at NHA Sofia, currently attending "Game design" masterclass at the Academy. Right now I'm working as a character artist in Ubisoft Sofia.
